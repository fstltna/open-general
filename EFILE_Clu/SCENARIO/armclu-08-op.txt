Struggle For The Bridgeheads - Aurith

02/12/1945

South of Frankfurt (Oder), the Soviets appeared on the banks of the Oder at Aurith and crossed the river to establish a bridgehead at Vogelsang, north of Fuerstenberg. German Ad Hoc units were thrown into desperate counterattacks but failed to dislodge the Soviets.

New units have strength 5 and experience 299! First scenario with Soviet air appearance.
