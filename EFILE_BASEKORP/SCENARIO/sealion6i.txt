Portsmouth

Orders:

The Isle of Wight is ours! Now we can set up our operations against the important ports of Portsmouth and Southampton. You have to take both cities. Expect british naval vessels in The Solent and Southampton Water.

Historical info:

During the second world war Portsmouth and its port were bombed by the Luftwaffe 67 times between July 1940 and May 1944. Portsmouth was an important embarkation point for the D-Day landings and the allied headquarters was just outside the city.
