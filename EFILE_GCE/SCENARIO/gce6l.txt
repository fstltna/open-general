The battle has been horrible... and more horrible has been the retreat. After the fall of Teruel, the rebels have overrun our troops. Maybe this strike against El Ejercito Popular has sealed our fate. In spite of your failure here, the high command, and the Republic, still need your services. For all our sakes, you must succeed next time.
