
VERMILION BAY - 9 Oct 1945

HQ 1st Parachute Army, Arkansas
General Kurt Student

General, our lightning campaign against the USA is going well. Nevertheless, we receive information's that the US Army in her despaired situation plans an attack on our accumulations of troop with newly developed medium-range missiles.
Suspicious activities were announced from south Louisiana at Vermilion Bay. Jump with your men directly into the missile positions and destroy these dangerous rockets.

AUXIN - Air-landing troops, Air- & Naval-Support
