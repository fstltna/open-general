
RAID ON BAKU - 6 May 1944

Azerbaijan-Iran-Border Region
CO General Vittorio Ambrosio

General, after our success in Iraq and the war entrance of the Turkish nation on our side, we have now the option to attack the Russians from the south. An attack on the Caucasus will force the Russians on his knees. Start an attack on Baku and secure there Oil for us.

AUXIN: 1 Armoured Division
