TACTICAL VICTORY

You did well but the Germans were stronger than expected. They defended feriously. Although your goals have been taken the British cancel the operation to push on. You will have to dig in again and problaby wait for the war to end.

AFTERMATH:
On the 5th of May all German troops the Netherlands, Denmark and north Germany surrender. The Netherlands are free once again. Your force is already being sent to Australia once again. From there you join the Australian invasion fleet at Morotai heading for Tarakan in the Dutch East Indies which will be assaulted early in May 1945.
