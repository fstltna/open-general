Tallinn Training
June 1, 1917

Kapten, you are the Executive Officer of the 1st Estonian regiment being formed in Tallinn under supervision of Imperial Russian authorities.  In this training exercise you will co-ordinate the actions of the battalions directly under your command with the maneuver of elements of the Russian 425th regiment.

Your first task is to hold a defensive position at Rapta.  Two German groups, the KaiserGrupe and the BismarckGrupe, will assault.  Form an assault group and, with the help of Russian reinforcements, strike the German flank and to capture Kella.  Also be aware of any German cavalry activity on your own flanks as you move toward your final objective SW of Rapta.
