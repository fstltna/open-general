
REVENGE - 8 OCT 1942

HQ Soviet Partisans, Latvia
CO General Karotamm

Parts of an SS-Division were moved in our area for rest and refreshing. It is a unit well-known as Anti-Partisan combat formation and has spread terror among the Soviet population.
This is our chance for revenge! Your force should be strong enough to enclose and wipe out this devils. Especially the leaders of this bunch should receive no mercy.

Order: Search & destroy three enemy unit. The units can be identified by an extra frame around their unit strength.
