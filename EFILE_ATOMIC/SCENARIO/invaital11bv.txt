Historical Info:

The Brazilian Expeditionary Force fought in Italy from September 1944 to May 1945. The BEF fought at the Gothic Line and in the 1945 final offensive in northern Italy. Brazil was the only South American country to send ground troops to fight overseas. It depolyed none of its tanks, which stayed at the home front, but 48 pilots, equiped with P-47D Thunderbolts flew combat missions in Italy. Overall, more than 25.000 brazilian soldiers fought in Italy, while the navy and air force took part in the Battle of the Atlantic from 1942 onwards.
