
NANCHANG COUNTEROFFENSIVE
25 Apr 1939

Part of Battle of Nanchang
Mid-February - Early May 1939

On March 27, Japanese forces made a violent attack against Nanchang. The Chinese fought bitterly against the Japanese in street fighting and suffered heavy casualties. But at night, Nanchang fell. On April 23, the Chinese 32nd Army Group began the counterattack against Nanchang without success. On May 9, the Chinese forces stop the attacks, and the entire front returned to what it had been.
