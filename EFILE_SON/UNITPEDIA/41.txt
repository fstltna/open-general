The Hungarian word literally means “8-pounder Cannon”.

It is a Large-Caliber Battery, with meium range, high attack and weak defense values. It has three special skills: it can capture a flag hex, it can attack naval targets and it can use rail transport (RTP).

This is a fictional unit created to make the series of the field guns complete. In reality, the Hungarian Army had only 6 and 12 pdr field guns.
