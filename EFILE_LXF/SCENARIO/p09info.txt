09. GUSTAV LINE (7 June 1944)

The Allied army is Italy is composed of by nationalities. Americans, British, Canadians, Indians, Poles, Frenchmen, Brazilian, Greek and even Portuguese troops join the struggle against the Germans. For months the enemy was able to hold our advance at the so called Gustav Line with the famous Monte Cassino position. Recently the Germans have been forced to retreat! The advance is about to begin again!

The PEF has been deployed in the mountains near the eastern, Adriatic coast under command of British 8th Army. Together with Indian, Polish and Canadian units you are ordered to conduct a general advance with the aim of capturing Pescara. The Germans are exhausted but still have good defensive postitions and are eager to fight. Good luck, General!
