Battle of Viipuri
Due to the invasion of west allied troops in France the Germans have withdrawn many troops. Your next operation will be the attack in Karelia. Move forward and capture Viipuri. 
Your troops:
You will only have your tank army for this mission. 
Enemy troops:
You are facing several finish troops. However, you should expect that the Germans will help Finland with some German reinforcements. Nonetheless, the enemy troops should be no problem for you.
