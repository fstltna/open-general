Scapa Flow

Orders:

Your orders are to take command of U-47 and to infiltrate Scapa Flow, the home of the Royal Navy. If you manage to stay undetected, you should be able to find and destroy a large target. Good luck, commander!

Historical Info:

U-47 penetrated Scapa Flow on 14 October 1939 and sunk the battleship HMS Royal Oak. Commander Günther Prien recieved the Knight's Kross for this raid. U-47 went on to become one of the most successful U-Boats of WWII. While still in command, Prien and U-47 with its entire crew of 45 men disapperead in the North Atlantic on 7th March 1941. Until today it is not known what happend to U-47 on this day.
