Crossing the River Stokhid
(October 12, 1915)

General Falkenhayn has the German troops carrying out an enveloping movement in the area of Minsk, while the Austro-Hungarian troops have been ordered to take Równe, an important transportation hub south of the Pripyat Marshes. However, the Russians are suspected to have built strong defense lines after their great retreat. Your job is to explore the eastern bank of Stokhid River.

ORDER: Take all VHs and keep your MSU alive.
