French conquest of Morocco - Sidi Bou Othmane - 6th, Sep. 1912 - BV

Colonel Bertaux-Levillain, as usual, you were successful. Soon you'll be promoted to  général. But now you must come back to France. It seems that the international situation is becoming more and more difficult.

HISTORICAL EVENTS:
Experience and superior firepower soon led the french to victory. Marrakech fall into French hands again, after a few hours of battle.
