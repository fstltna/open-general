1937-43 IMPERIAL JAPANESE NAVY

A semi-historical campaign about the Imperial Japanese Navy and especially the Special Naval Landing Forces in China and World War II. 
You're a fan of naval warfare in the Pacific, amphibious landing operations and naval aviation? If so, you should play this campaign!
