Take Szekesfehervar!
While the Germans try to break through our lines at the Lake Balaton in the west, your mission is to break through the German and Hungarian line at Szekesfehervar. With the capture of the town the way to Vienna will be open.
Your troops:
You will only have your tank army for this mission.
Enemy troops:
Your opponent is the Army Group Balck. The troops are battle-wearied, but they have some serious tanks and fighters. 
!!!Please notice!!!: 
The next two scenarios will be no-auto-refit. So, you should ensure that your troops are in acceptably conditions at the end of the battle.
Sgn. Major Heinz
