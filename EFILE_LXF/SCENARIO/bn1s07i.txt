
LENINGRAD FRONT - 6 Nov 1941

HQ Army Group North, Russia
General Wilhelm Ritter von Leeb

Welcome to the Leningrad Front, General Major! The Russians are attempting to break through to the east with strong forces. At present we have no reserves for a counterattack. Stop the attacks with your division! The front must be held!

AUXIN - 3 Infantry divisions & Air-Support
HINT   - Let the enemy come!
