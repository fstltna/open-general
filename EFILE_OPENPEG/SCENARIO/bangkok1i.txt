Bangkok, Thailand

Our navies have managed to thwart enemy operations in the South China Sea, but victory for our forces on land requires a firm foundation to operate from. You are to establish defensive positions east of the Nang Rang River, then assault Bangkok and secure the rail lines north along the Chayo Praya River to Saraburi.
