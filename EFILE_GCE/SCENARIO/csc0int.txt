Alcazar de Toledo (Toledo)

26 de Septiembre de 1936

Buenos dias general!. Nuestra marcha victoriosa desde el Protectorado, hacia la capital continua. Las hordas rojas aun campan a sus anchas por Madrid, pero seguro que por poco tiempo. A pesar de ello, las ordenes son que cambie la direccion de su columna; el general Mola ha enviado ordenes de que debe liberar el Alcazar de Toledo, donde la guarnicion que se ha sublevado con nosotros, al mando del coronel Moscardo esta resistiendo desesperadamente los asaltos de los milicianos enemigos.

El Alcazar se ha convertido en todo un simbolo para la Espana Nacional, asi que su deber es liberar el Alcazar lo antes posible, ademas su guarnicion esta al borde de la derrota.

Tiene usted 1000 puntos de prestigio para construir su columna.

Buena suerte, general.


Alcazar de Toledo (Toledo)

September 26th, 1936

Buenos dias general!. Our victorious march from the Spanish Morocco to Madrid continues. The red hordes are still ruling Madrid... but surely for a few time. In spite of this fact, you should change the direction of your troops; the general Mola have ordered you to save the Alcazar de Toledo, (an infantry school is located there) where the garrison is desperately resisting the attacks of the reds since July, receiving heavy assaults from the CNT militias which are besieging the Alcazar.

The Alcazar has become to be a symbol of our resistance to the enemy, so your task is;

...Save the garrison of the Alcazar at any costs and as soon as possible!!!!, the Alcazar must not fall into the enemy hands, and the garrison (consisting on officers of the infantry school, managed by the colonel Moscardo) is nearly to be defeated.

You have 1000 prestige available to build up your army.

Buena suerte, general.
