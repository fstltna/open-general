Fall Weiss [14. Armee]

Fall Weiss, Poland 1939

-Fourteenth Army, Army Group South-

-General List-

September 1939, Germany finally has broken the relationships with Poland, the invasion is on the way. The Polish will try to resist with an outdated army against the fast German Panzerdivisionen. With the Fourteenth German Army, you will have under your command the next units; the 5th Panzer Division, the SS unit ''Germania'', the 8.Infaterie Division and a Engineer unit. Your first mission as a commander of the 14. Armee, will be the assault on Krakow, one of the more industrialized areas of Poland.

4 scenarios.
