
SHENNONG MOUNTAINS - 22 May 1943

Part of Battle of Western Hupei
(Chinese victory)
Early May - Mid-June 1943

HQ 11th Corps, Province Hupei
CO General Isamu Yokoyama

General, we are in a very unfavourable position at I-chang.  Since the Chinese occupation around I-chang we have come under endless counterattacks.  This must be stopped.  We must crush the enemy forces in western Hupei and clear the navigational traffic in the upper reaches of the Yangtze River.  Banzai!

ORDER - Capture all VHs!
AUXIN - 2 Infantry-Battalions
