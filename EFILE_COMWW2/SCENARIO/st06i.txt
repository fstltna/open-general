November 19 1942

Herr General: Stavka has surprised us completely by attacking our flanks in great force! Our Romanian allies cannot resist for long. The situation is desperate. You, and some of your men, have been ordered here to the Chir River to lend any assistance you can to our faltering allies...

VICTORY CONDITIONS:

Retain control of the outpost at Plessistyi and Exit 4 units at the bridge near Ostroff...
