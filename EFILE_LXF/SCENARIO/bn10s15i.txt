
KHANAQIN OILFIELDS - 15 JUL 1941

Baghdad, Iraq

After your success and short rest at Baghdad, you will perform a attack towards Khanaqin into northeastern direction at the Iranian border. There are together with Mosul the largest oil fields of Iraq and Indian troops preparing the destruction of the production facilities. This must be prevented at all costs, act fast!

GOAL: Take all VH's and prevent the destruction of 1 of 3 oil wells (MSUs)!
