08. Operation Wop - March 20th, 1943

Montgomery started his attack on the Mareth Line. First Armoured is to support him with a limited attack, taking Sened followed by Maknassy and the mountain pass East of Maknassy. 60th Infantry Regiment of the 9th Infantry Division is attached to your division for this attack.

ORDERS
Take all Victory Hexes. 

Expect no reinforcements. Three units can be deployed West of Sened. The rest at the Supply Hex.

NOTES: 
- Units will be automatically reinforced to strength 10 at the end of the scenario and you will be able to acquire new units at that point.
