
CLEVELAND - 3 AUG 1944

Ashtabula, Ohio, United States

After your forces have now reached the Lake Erie, turn your forces to southwest and start the attack on Cleveland. Cleveland is one of the largest cities in the United States and several enemy divisions are ready to defend the city. Southeast of Cleveland is a large ammunition depot of the U.S. Army, the Ravenna Arsenal. Also this object is one of your attack targets!

GOAL: Hold 15/12/10 VH's for a BV/V/TV!

NOTE: All axis nations auxiliaries are under your command.
