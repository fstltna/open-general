
TABRIZ - 11 OCT 1941

Hashtrood, Iran

After the heavy defeat at Tehran the Soviets withdraw from Iran. The retreat runs relatively orderly but slow. If we act quickly, it might be possible to cut off some Soviet divisions at Tabriz. Attack with your forces and interrupt the Tabriz - Jolfa rail link.

GOAL: Take all VH's and prevent the retreat of more than 4 enemy trains via Exit Point!

NOTE: All axis nations auxiliaries are under your command.

Main reinforcements:
6th turn - southwest, Iranian Regiment-size combat group
