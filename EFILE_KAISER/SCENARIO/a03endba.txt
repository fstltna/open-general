OUTCOME

With the Czech action at Tsjeljabinsk the imprisoned Legionairs were freed. Even more, the Soviet rule in the city was destroyed. The Legion would take care of their own way how to reach Wladiswostok and get out of Russia. No longer it would cooperate with the Soviet authorities. Still the Legion was scattered from Penza, west of the Volga River, to Tsjeljabinsk, east of the Urals...
