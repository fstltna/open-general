Retreat from Tabora

In 1916 a Belgian army, supported by some British units, invaded German East Africa as part of the Allied operations which sought to conquer the last of German territories in Africa. However the German defenses were cleverly organized by General Wahle and put up a ferocious resistance. 

Victory conditions: Retreat a total of 8 German units.

If the Allied Army takes Usoke on the first 6 turns, you will lose.
