Marasesti- August, 1917 - TV

What a pity, Oberstleutnant. Although you did accomplish the basic goals of your mission, you weren’t able to exploit your success, and our enemies retreated to safer positions, where they’ll establish a powerful defence line.

Well, I guess it doesn’t matter. Russia is in the middle of a revolution, and seems determined to destroy herself. We’ll gladly help her to do that. Sooner or later the Russian revolutionaries will have to ask for peace, and Romania will follow. It will take longer than we desired, but finally, after so many sacrifices, the victory in the Eastern front will be a reality.
