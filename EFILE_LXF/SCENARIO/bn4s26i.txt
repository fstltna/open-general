
ARIDAEA - 14 DEC 1948

East of Aridaea, West Macedonia

The liberation of Greek cities is still at the top of our priorities. Your Division will carry out an surprise attack across the Yugoslav border on Aridaea and free the city! If the resistance is broken here, the further advance on Veroea and Edessa is planned. Let's hope that you succeed and meet an unprepared enemy!

GOAL: Take all VHs!

+++++++++++++++++++++++++++++++++++++++++++++++++++++++

STORY: Between december 15-30, 1948, the 10th DSE division under Kapetan Gousias attacks on the towns of Veroea, Edessa & Aridaea in North Greece. The division suffered many losses & withdrew to Vitsi.
