TACTICAL VICTORY

You have witnissed the tenacity of the Japanese when it comes to fighting - defensively or offensively, nonetheless you prevailed! Very well done. Brigadier Blackburn, commander of Blackforce, sends a word of thanks to you and your men which saved the day. The Japanese Nasu Detachment is being slowed down as well as other Japanese formations marching directly to Batavia, but the Japanese are far from defeated. You have orders to pull back in darkness to the east where you will wait for the Japanese to come.
