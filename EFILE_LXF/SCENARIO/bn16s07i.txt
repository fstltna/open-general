
LEEWARD ISLANDS - 20 FEB 1937

North off Leeward Islands, Caribbean Sea

Commander in Chief, Atlantic Fleet informs
A large-scale operation to secure the Panama Canal and capture of the British West Indian colonial territories - British Honduras, British Guiana, Jamaica, Leeward Islands etc. has started.
Your task is to capture the islands of Montserrat and Dominica. The 93rd Army Brigade is subordinated under your command for that. The Brits have enhanced the defense of the islands last time and further reinforcements, especially for Dominica, are on the way.
Stop further transports of troops and weapons to Dominica and take the islands quickly!

The light cruiser USS Memphis is deployed under your command. Congratulation!
