
BATTLE OF TSAOYANG
6 May 1940

Part of Battle of Tsaoyang-I-chang
Early May - Mid-June 1940

Having been dealt a heavy blow by Chinese forces in the Winter Offensive, the Japanese in Wuhan increasingly felt the threat of Chinese forces in Tahung Shan and Tungpo Shan. He was unable to make use of the rice bins of the Chiang-Han Plains. During the First Phase Operations the Japanese regarded Tsaoyang as his advance objective.
