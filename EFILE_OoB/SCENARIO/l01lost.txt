LOSS

Under your command 22. Infanterie Division has not succeeded in capturing its objectives. However the total operation is saved by the Dutch surrender which was forces by the massive bombing of Rotterdam and our 18. Armee closing in. The airborne operation did not succeed and resulted in many losses but you are not to blame for this.


AFTERMATH

Your division gets rest and refitting as part of the occupation force in Belgium and Holland. January 1941 the division is back in Germany but in April already the division is brought to Rumania as part of the German military mission to that country.
