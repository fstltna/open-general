Encounter with the Boii 

Boiorix - King of the Cimbri - continue towards the south. The tribes of  Teutons and Ambrones will accompany you. You first meet the Boii - a Celtic tribe.

They will not give up their land voluntarily. It is therefore necessary to conquer the land of the Boii.
Warriors! Go on the attack and bring honor to all of us!
