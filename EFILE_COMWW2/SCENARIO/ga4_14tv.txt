Despite fierce resistance, and the remaining intact of the German line of defence on the Narew River in the Lomza area, Stalin has noted the capture of the fortress of Osowiec and sends congratulations to the units and commanders involved!


November 16 1944

Comrade Marshal, STAVKA has directed that you be informed that you have now been assigned to direct command of the 1st Belorussian front, while Marshal Rossokovsky has been appointed commander of 2nd Belorussian front, effective immediately...
