Woronesch - 06-07-1942

German summer offensive has just started. Armeegruppe von Weichs is about
to pass Woronesch and move towards Stalingrad. Feldmarschall von Bock
wants to take the chance and capture Woronesch without explicit order.

4. Panzerarmee is expected to get into heavy fights for the railway bridge west
of the city. On 13-07-1942 russian troops launch an attack from the north with
200+ tanks. Stop their offensive and take all victory hexes.

Heavy artillery is expected to defend the city.

Take the 3 russian victory hexes.
