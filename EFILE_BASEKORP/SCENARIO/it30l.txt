GAZA July 1943 L

The Allied green forces proved too numerous and our advance has come to a standstill once more.

With the German attack against the Kursk Salient halted after an inconclusive tank battle at Prokhorovka and the Allies on the offensive at the Solomon Islands against the Japanese, the Axis have lost the initiative in the war. With more and more US forces being shipped into battle, victory seems further away than ever before.
