No Price too High

1941-45

On September 10th 1939, well aware that the coming conflict would be long and demanding, Canada joined Britain and the other Commonwealth countries in declaring war on Germany. It would be some time before Canadian forces would fully be able to participate.The Canadian government's first priority was to build an army, navy, and airforce from practically nothing, and then prepare to fight for the righteous and just cause the young nation had committed to. 
Fielding an armed forces composed mainly of volunteers, the Canadians went on to prove that for the defeat of the Axis powers, no price was too high...
