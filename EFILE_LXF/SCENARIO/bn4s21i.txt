
BATTLE OF AGORELITSA - 17 JUL 1944

South of Korifasi, Peloponnese

You will carry out a strike against the German occupation forces on the western Peloponnese. Target of your attack is the 41st Fortress Division and the neutralization of the coastal artillery near Marathopoli. The Germans have some battle-hardened units in this area, so be careful!

GOAL: Take all VHs!

NOTE: The unit occupying the town Agorelitsa, gets the chance of a Leader!
