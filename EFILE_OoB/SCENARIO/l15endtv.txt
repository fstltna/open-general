TACTICAL VICTORY

Your division has reached Cilli and orders from Korps command has been received to stop fighting and surrender. Your division has held high the honours and tradition of the German Heer!

On 14 May 1945 the 22. Infanterie Division surrenders to the Yugoslav armies and goes into Yugoslav captivity. The division was one of the oldest division of the German Wehrmacht and was an example of the professionality of the German Army.
