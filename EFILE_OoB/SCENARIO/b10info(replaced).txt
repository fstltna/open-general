02. PLAYER'S CHOICE SCENARIO (2 January 1945)

With the capture of Pearl Ridge the central sector has been secured. The main operations will now be executed on the southern and northern sectors. You may choose which sector you will be fighting.

--------------------------

In order to play the NORTHERN PATH you have to capture the NORTHERN VICTORYHEX. The northern path will lead you towards Buka on the northern tip of Bougainville.

In order to play the SOUTHERN PATH you have to capture the SOUTHERN VICTORYHEX. The southern path will lead you towards Buin on the southern tip of Bougainville.

--------------------------
