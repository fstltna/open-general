AC 102 - The First Battle of Aquae Sextiae

The Romans followed you, Teutobod. Your rearguard - a squad of Ambrones - have the Romans behind their backs... reach the secure storage with 15 men. But keep the strategically important parts of the hill and prepare for the decisive battle...

Notes of historical interest:

When drawing water, the Romans light Ligurian auxiliaries encountered the Teutons rearguard, consisting mainly of Ambrones. The fighting soon spread to a battle from where the Romans gained the upper hand after a fierce battle. They pursued the retreating Teutons until they holed up in their laager.
