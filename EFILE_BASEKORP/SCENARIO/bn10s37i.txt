
OAK RIDGE - 4 AUG 1943

Cleveland, Tennessee, United States

On your march route west of Knoxville should exist a secret town called Site X. According to our intelligence the Americans operate a nuclear research facility there. It would be desirable that this facility falls intact into our hands. Commando units were tasked to occupy this facility by surprise and to arrest the scientists and technicians. Once the facility is in our hand you must ensure that the enemy is not able to destroy the facility with artillery or his air force!

GOAL: Take all VH's and ensure that 3 of 5 of your MSUs survive!

NOTE: In this mission exceptionally the Americans makes the first move!
