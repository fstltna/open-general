LOSS

The German did not only break through in other parts of the country but their paratroopers captured all important targets without help of their main force. Rotterdam has been bombed and the city is in ruins. General Winkelman will sign the capitulation of all Dutch forces in the Netherlands except Zeeland soon. The war is lost...

CREDITS: SCENARIO IS BASED UPON JURGEN SMET'S STUDENT SCENARIO WHICH IS BEING USED IN HIS "DAS REICH" CAMPAIGN.
