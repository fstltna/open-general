Open General - Read Me First

Welcome to Open General
After installing for first time, you can download further updates from Open General Daily Build where you can eventually find new campaigns and equipment files that are released after your installation.You can susbcribe to check for updates easily

For further information and latest updates, visit: Luis's Tools site where you can get:

  • An overview of game features and additional optional packages to download: Main Open General page
  • Advanced executable and change log page , including a link to download the advanced version of the game executable, and a full report of all changes made.
    This advanced version only includes the executable, and is available before installer is updated, so if you reported any bug and you know it is fixed, this is the only package you need to update.
  • Guille's Open General manual:Open General English Manual  (pdf)
  • The "school" pdf themes: Open General School page
  • Blitzstoper advanced: Tactical Manual for OpenGeneral players with very useful hints related with campaigns

FORUM.
Most of the active comunity players and designers are members of JP's OpenGen Forum , so it is worth to join in order to check questions raised by other players and post your own questions, as well as to know current work in progress by designers.
Also can check JP's PG2 Challenge Campaign Forum to enjoy the experience of playing same campaign with other players, a very instructive practive to master Open General.
