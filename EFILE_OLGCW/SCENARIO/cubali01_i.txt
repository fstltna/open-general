Sierra Maestra 1958

After one year of the first guerrilla actions agaisnt Batista, the power of the cuban rebels leaded by Castro is growing each day. Castro thoughts the land controled by the  M-26-7 movement had to be increased in the Sierra Maestra. However, he didn't know about the Batista intentions to launch a powerful strike on the Sierra, to clear the southern part of Cuba from rebels.
