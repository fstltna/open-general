The Cimbri and Teutonic migration

113 BC

Presumably due to environmental changes

Probably due to environmental changes and the resulting crop failures, the Cimbri and
Teutons – residents of Jutland – heeded traders' reports concerning the fertile fields and pastures of the south, and moved there.
But here are the areas of the Celts and Romans. 

Take over the control of Teutobod and the Teutons or Boiorix and the Cimbris and become master of central Europe.
