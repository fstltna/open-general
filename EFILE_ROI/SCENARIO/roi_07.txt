Isjum - 12-05-1942

German plans to launch operation Fridericus on 18-05-1942 were crossed when
Timoschenko attacked with his 6th and 57th army out of the Isjum pocket on 
12-05-1942.

Armeegruppe von Kleist tries to cut off russian operations with 1. Panzerarmee and
parts of the 17th army. Encircle the russian troops and take all victory hexes.
Some romanian troops are there for your support.
