OPERATION BREVITY May 1941 - L

The British Operation is a complete success. Gott has taken posession of Halfaya and Fort Capuzzo. The British forces are in an excellent starting position for an offensive towards Tobruk. Rommel has decided to retreat to Gazala. 

News: German and Italian troops have captured Greece. The attack on Crete will start on the 20th of this month.
