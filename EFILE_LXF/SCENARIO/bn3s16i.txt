
BURMA ROAD - 3 May 1942

Operations along the Yunnan-Burma Road
(Japanese victory)
Mid-March - Early June 1942

HQ 15th Corps, Burma
CO General Shojiso Ida

General, with the fall of Rangoon it is only a matter of time until we liberate Burma.  Intelligence indicates that three divisions of Chinese troops have crossed the border to support the British.  Our attack can not be stalled.  Attack to the north with all speed!

ORDER - Capture all VHs!
AUXIN - 3 Battalions
