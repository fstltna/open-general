
OPERATION WESER EXERCISE - 9 Apr 1940

HQ XXI Army Corps, Germany
General Nikolaus von Falkenhorst

We will forestall the Allies and occupy Denmark and Norway. Your Job is to conquer the airport Fornebu east from Oslo. Advance then on the capital and secure the city and harbour. Good luck, Major.

AUXIN - 2 Infantry-Regiments, Air- & Naval-Support.
HINT   - Infantry in air transport can be landed on airfields
