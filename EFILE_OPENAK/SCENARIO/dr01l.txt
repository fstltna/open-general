Panzer-Verbaende Ostpreussen Loss

Your less than spectacular performance has motivated a reevaluation of your usefulness.   Some of the enemy has retreated into Romania.  The British and French have now declared war on Germany.  If we did not need every man to fight, your career would be over!

HISTORY
Panzer Division Kempf fought more battles with the Poles at Rozan, Lomza, Kliczym and Modlin. The division was disbanded after the Polish campaign at a farewell parade near Neidenburg at October 7th 1939.
