
PORTSMOUTH - 16 AUG 1940

Southeast off Portsmouth, England

Operation Sea Lion the battle for Britain has begun! You have been provided the best equipment we have but sadly all your troops lack battle experience.

Your battle group will land at dawn along with the 26th Infantry Division at Portsmouth. This area form the westernmost section of the invasion front and your goal is to take the Port of Portsmouth and to reach Southampton in the west. The landing of the second wave, in your section the 4th Panzer-Division, is scheduled 24 hours after the start of the invasion.

Mine the harbor entrance of Portsmouth and occupy or build as soon as possible an airfield.

GOAL: Take all VH's!

Main reinforcements:
5th turn - south, 4th Panzer-Division
