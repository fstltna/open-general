Rzhev-Vyazma Operation - Februari 1942 V

After the Russian 39rd Army, 29th Army and the 11th Cavalry Corps, now also the 33rd Army is encircled. You have stopped the Russian Winter offensive. Congratulations. 
A Kamfgruppe of your division will stay on the Eastern front until June 1st, but you are ordered to move to France to prepare the reorganization of Das Reich into a Panzer-Grenadier division.
