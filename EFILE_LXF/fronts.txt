* ******************************************************************
* FRONTS (#) all nations possess:
* ******************************************************************

* @NONE - No units available!
* ---------------------------------
* No units will be available.


* ******************************************************************
* FRACTIONS (@) all nations possess:
* ******************************************************************

* @NAVY + Corps
* ---------------------------------
* All NAVY ground units of the respective nation, which are corps
* or army units. 


* @NAVY + Naval
* ---------------------------------
* All naval units of the respective nation and all aircraft that
* can be carried by ships. Except aircraft carriers and aircraft 
* that can be carried by aircraft carriers.


* @NAVY + Aviation
* ---------------------------------
* All aircraft of the respective nations NAVY.


* @NAVY + Infantry
* ---------------------------------
* All NAVY ground units of the respective nation, which are not
* corps or army units.


* @NAVY + Coastal
* ---------------------------------
* All naval units of the respective nation with movement NAVAL or
* COASTAL. These units can navigate on rivers and shallow water.  


* @-
* ---------------------------------
* Not to be used!


* ******************************************************************
* FRACTIONS (@) some nations possess:
* ******************************************************************

* @NAVY + Carriers + Aviation
* ---------------------------------
* All aircraft carriers of the respective nations NAVY and aircraft 
* that can be carried by aircraft carriers.
