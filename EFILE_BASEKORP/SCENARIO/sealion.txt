16. Operation Sealion

Version:  Feb- 2019
Number of Scenarios: 18
Player Country: Germany
Equipment efile: BaseKorp
Campaign Designer: randowe
Modified by Brian Gosley aka Puma

This campaign will take you from the cliffs of Scapa Flow to the White Cliffs of Dover. You'll invade Great Britain and experience adventures on the north american continent.

Notes:
Paratroopers and airborne sappers are very important in this campaign.
In many scenarios you'll start without an airfield. You'll have to build one.
Do not sell free core naval vessels. You'll need them!
Read the scenario description and turn messages carefuly.
Free refit mode.
