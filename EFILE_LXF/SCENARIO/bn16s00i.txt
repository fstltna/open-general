
NAUTILUS INCIDENT - 3 AUG 1936

East off Newfoundland, North Atlantic

Commander in Chief, Atlantic Fleet informs
One of our submarines, SS-168 the Nautilus discovered British warships east of your position and shadowed the British now. Due to the massive tensions between Washington and London and the aggressive behavior of the Royal Navy last time, you have order to prepare your units to fight. Continue your combat exercises, but be alert at all times to respond to aggressive behavior by the British.
