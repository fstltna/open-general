Kiev encirclement - Romny September1941

The Russians attempt to relieve their encircled forces at Kiev. You must respond with a counter attack.

The 10 Motorized Infantry Division is ordered to assist your attack from the North asap, followed soon by the 7th Panzer Division which will support your attack from the South.

PLAYER ORDERS
- Strength lost on core units will be automatically replaced at HQ at the start of the next scenario.
- New units can be acquired at HQ at the start of the next scenario.
- The supply hex allows you to deploy all the allowed core forces, but not all on the first turn.
