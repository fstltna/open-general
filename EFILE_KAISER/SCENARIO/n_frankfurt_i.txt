1918 The revolt comes upon Frankfurt

7 November

The revolution is fomented. The coastal towns are in our hands. Now freed Frankfurt on Main and  conquer by this the city of Finance, or can Groﬂherzog Ernst Ludwig of Hessen put down the rebellion?

Historical:
According to the Sailors' revolt in Kiel, the revolution spreads to Frankfurt

There they formed Workers' and Soldiers' Councils and called for the abdication of Grand Duke Ernst Ludwig of Hesse. But he refuses ...


Autor = Matze (M. Kessler)
