The title says it all, actually:  Lots and lots of small tanks buzzing around, and the AI will buy more as time passes.

Do you ignore them and race for the Victory Hexes, or do you try to get a handle on the situation first? Your decision.

There are (almost) no (bad) surprises in this map, just the usual Bonus Bunkers. Nevertheless you get another freebie, a mobile AD you might need as enemy bombers get more powerful.
