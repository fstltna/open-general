
LEROS
12 Nov 1943

Dodecanese: 8th of September 1943, Marshal Badoglio communicates the Italian surrender. Everywhere in Italy and the Balkan were the Italian troops disarmed by the Germans. But on the Dodecanese islands, the Italian garrison changes the side to the Allies. The German attack on the island Leros began on 12th November with naval landings and the drop of paratroopers. After heavy fighting the garrison surrendered on 16th November.
