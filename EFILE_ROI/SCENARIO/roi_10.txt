Operation Uranus - 19-11-1942

German forces at Stalingrad where making the final attempt to clear the city,
when STAVKA launched an counterattack out of the Serafimowitsch and Kletskaja
bridgeheads to cut off the german troops.

Romanian forces were overrun, only 1st cavalry corps and 6th infanterie division
under Mihail Lascar fought bravely. Try to stop the fast mechanized corps of the
5th tank army. Russian 21st army is expected to arrive from the east later.

Only a small portion of XXXVIII tank corps under General Heim is there for your
support.

Take all victory hexes neccessary.
