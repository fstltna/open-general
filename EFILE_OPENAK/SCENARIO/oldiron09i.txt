09. The drive on Tunis - The endgame - April 23th, 1943

Axis forces have retreated into Northern Tunisia and US II Corps has been shifted to the far North of the Allied line. The role of II Corps in the final campaign to kick the Axis out of Northern Africa is to capture Mateur and Bizerte.

ORDERS
Take all Victory Hexes. 

Expect no reinforcements. Three units can be deployed close to the front line. The rest at the Supply Hex further behind the front.

NOTES: 
- Units will be automatically reinforced to strength 10 at the end of the scenario and you will be able to acquire new units at that point.
