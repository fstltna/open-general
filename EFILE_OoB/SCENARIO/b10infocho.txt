02. PLAYER'S CHOICE SCENARIO (2 January 1945)

With the capture of Pearl Ridge, the central sector has been secured. The main operations will now be executed on the southern and northern sectors. You may choose which sector you will be fighting.

--------------------------

The northern path will lead you towards Buka on the northern tip of Bougainville.
11th Brigade will take care of operations in the northern sector.

The southern path will lead you towards Buin on the southern tip of Bougainville.
29th Brigade will take care of operation in the southern sector.

--------------------------
