Final Assault at Richmond * BV


----{secret telegraph depeche to general}-----
THE WHITE HOUSE; WASHINGTON,

Dear Sir,
Unbelievable, you made it !!!
You have taken Richmond and the enemy had to surrender !
The war is over ! We have won !
if you plan to run for presidency in the future, I could help you with advice...
God bless and long live liberty !

THE PRESIDENT OF THE UNITED STATES
-----{end of secret telegraph depeche}----



dear friend,
we have won, I fullfilled my duty: I resign as general, return to my school and teach young people again.....
there is one thing that I will teach them for sure: Their are times where freedom must be defended.... 
and here my friend, here ends my long journey to freedom.
