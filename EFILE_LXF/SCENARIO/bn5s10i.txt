
AUGUST STORM - 12 AUG 1945

HQ Transbaikal Front, Manchuria
CO General Malinovsky

Comrade, the liberation of Manchuria from Japanese occupation making good progress on all fronts. Outstanding work, but now the heavily defended area around Solon is our next target. A breakthrough in this area is necessary for our further advance! The high command is expecting a quick victory!

Order: Capture all VHs or destroy all enemy units!
