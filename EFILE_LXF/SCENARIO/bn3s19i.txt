
HENGYANG - 2 Aug 1944

Part of Battle of Changsha-Hengyang
(Decisive Japanese victory)
Late May - Early August 1944

HQ 11th Corps, Province Hunan
CO General Isamu Yokoyama

General, we are now ready for the second phase of our major campaign ICHI-GO!  Our troops are now near Hengyang.  You have the honour of taking Hengyang and successfully completing our operation TO-GO I.  Banzai!

ORDER - Capture & protect all VHs!
AUXIN - 1 Infantry-Brigade & Railway-Support
HINT   - Beware of the strong enemy Air Force!
