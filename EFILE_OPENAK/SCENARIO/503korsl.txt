05. KORSUN-FREIHEIT LOSS

Failure! Gruppe Stemmmerman is lost. 55,000 Germans are dead or in Soviet captivity.

HISTORY
The German relief force could not hold Hill 239. Stemmerman's breakout turned into a route. Only 12,000 of the 55,000 troops reached German lines. March brought new Soviet attacks that encircled 1st Pz.Armee with 503 sPzAbt. in Hube's pocket.
