"When Barbarossa commences, the world will hold its breath". Adolf Hitler

June 22, 1941. The largest military operation in history begins. More than 3 million axis soldiers attack the Soviet Union. The attack was directed in three directions, each assigned to an Army Group: North, Centre and South. The operation great successes, but failed to meet its final objectives. Now it's your time to try to succeed where the Axis armies failed.

This is a 50 scenario German campaign, divided in three independent paths, one each for Army Group Center, North and South.

Original campaign by Jorge Gabitto.

Conversion to OG and Composite Efile by Guille.
