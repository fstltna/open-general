02. Unternehmen Zitadelle - Rzhavets (7 October 1943)

Your Schwere Panzer Abteilung is atached to III Panzer Korps to increase its capability to push the Soviet defenders aside.

The task of III Pz Korps is to protect the right flank of 4th Panzer Armee. 4th Panzer Armee is the main force of the Southern prong to cut of the Kursk sailient. 

Progress of 4th Panzer Armee is good, but III Pz Korps has only just now forced its way accross the Donets. Make up the time lost and move North fast. Expect Soviet armored counter attacks. 

NOTES: 
- 7th Pz Division will become available in 2 days (turn 5).
- Take all victory hexes.
- Units will not be automatically reinforced to strength 10 at the end of the scenario.
