SITUATION:

After many quite months the British und US forces launched a invasion on Sicily. They already captured Pantelleria and Lampedusa and now use the little islands to launch their planes. 

You and your elite troops are ordered to hold the line, no matter what! Push the enemy back into the sea! If we succeed, the Battle of the Mediterranean will be won!

The USA have already landed at Gela while the British landed at the eastern coast.

ORDERS: 

Hold 8/6/4 VHs after 14 turns for a BV/V/TV.

NOTES:

You can buy up to 2 new units in this scenario.

AI will have the first turn!

UPGRADES:

You get 2500pp and should upgrade your units to the most modern equipment available.
