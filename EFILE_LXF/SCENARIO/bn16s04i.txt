
SAINT LAWRENCE CROSSING - 16 OCT 1936

Saint Lawrence River, Quebec

Commander in Chief, Atlantic Fleet informs
Our 3rd Army Corps needs help in crossing the Saint Lawrence River. The ports for embarking our troops are mined and our minesweepers need cover to do their job. Furthermore support the army with your ship artillery and air forces in capturing Quebec City.
