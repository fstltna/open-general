From Buzuluk to Prague

*****

A World War 2 non-fictional semi-historic campaign about Czechoslovaks on the East Front.

Starting as an infantry battalion, the Czechoslovak unit rose to the size of an army corps till the end of the war. The Czechoslovaks fought many engagements with the Germans, including several of the bloodiest battles in the history of WW2, most notably the Carpatho-Dukla Operation.

Efile version: OpenAK
Time frame: spring 1943 - spring 1945


May all victims of all wars rest in peace!
