
THIRD BATTLE OF CHANGSHA - 12 Jan 1942

Third Battle of Changsha
(Decisive Chinese victory)
Late December 1941 - Mid-January 1942

HQ 11th Corps, Province Hupei
CO General Yuiki Anami

General, our attack has stalled against the damned city of Changsha.  With your lighting victory at Hong Kong we want you to immediately reinforce our troops at Changsha.  Banzai!  A strong enemy counterattack from the west are expected!

ORDER - Capture & protect all VHs!
AUXIN - Naval-Support
