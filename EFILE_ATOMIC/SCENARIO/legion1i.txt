SITUATION: 

A communist force is about to land along the east coast of Mallorca. Our local forces are outnumbered! Try to hold as many objectives as possible until our forces from the west of the island arrive in the battle zone. Take care, powerful enemy battleships have been reported in the area! 
A axuiliary bomber is reported to come over from the mainland soon.

ORDERS:

Push the communists back into the sea and capture all VHs in 12/15/18/ turns for a BV/V/TV.

NOTE:

You can buy up to 3 new units in this scenario. It is recommended to buy a fighter and do some upgrades.

Attachments can be sold off later and you will get back the prestige.

Throughout the whole campaign you can buy/upgrade to talian units only.
