
THESSALONIKI - EVACUATION - 9 APR 1941

Thessaloniki, Central Macedonia

Thessaloniki is not to hold - Evacuate your troops to Athens via sea immediately! The remains of the Krussia Detachment and the 19th Motorised Infantry Division try to kept clear your back as long as possible!

GOAL: Retreat 9 ground units (in sea transport) via Exit Points or hold after the seventh turn 4 VHs for BV! Hold after the seventh turn 3 VHs or 2 VHs for V or TV!

NOTE: Due to the short time between the missions at HQ no new units can be recruited!
