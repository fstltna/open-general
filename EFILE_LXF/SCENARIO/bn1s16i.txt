
BERLIN - 26 Apr 1945

HQ Army Group Weichsel, Berlin
General Gotthard Heinrici

General! Your paratroopers are our last hope! You must take over the defence of section VII and VIII in the north of Berlin. Hold your positions! Army WENCK in the southwest as well as the Combat Group STEINER in the north have orders to advance on Berlin. Your order: stand or die!

AUXIN - 2 Tank-Divisions & Air-Support
HINT  - Withdraw into the city and let the enemy come!
