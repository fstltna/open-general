AC 072 - Spartacus or Krixos

We are writing the Year 72 BC. The Celts are getting more and more aggressive and want to defeat Rome. For that Reason, Krixos seperates from Spartacus and takes his own way. 

Now it is your choice to go either with Spartacus or Krixos.

Historical: 
As described, a Group of Celts under the command of Sparatcus Friend and Subleader "Krixos" indeed seperated to keep going to attack the Romans in their own Country. Spartacus still followed his target to cross the Alps from Italy.
