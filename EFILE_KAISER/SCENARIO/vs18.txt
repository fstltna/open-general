2nd Ypres - Sint-Juliaan - April 24nd 1915

A new chlorine gas attack will be released on the Canadian First Division on your left flank to remove the threat on your advancing troops. Your tasks are to consolidate the gains made the last two days and to support the attack of 2.Reserve Ersatz Brigade, 38.Ldw. Brigade and Brigade Schmieden (a composite brigade of 53.Reserve Division batallions) on the Canadians.

Player orders: Capture all victory hexes.

Note: The gas units will disappear in turn 2, so make use of them straight away.
