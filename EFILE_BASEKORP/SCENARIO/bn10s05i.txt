
COLERAINE - 2 NOV 1940

Ballymena, North Ireland

Dublin and Belfast are taken by our troops and the British have started the evacuation of their remaining troops to Canada. We have order to prevent the evacuation via Londenderry and Coleraine in the north of the island. Your troops will advance on Coleraine in the northeast and stop these activities. Hurry up, it should not escape too many troops!

GOAL: Take all VH's and prevent that more than 5 enemy troopships reach the open sea (Exit Points)!
