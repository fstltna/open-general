Attack on Riga
After our success in Karelia we now want to free the Baltic states. Your tank army have the mission to attack the Germans in Riga and throw them out of the city.
Your troops:
You will only have your tank army for this mission. 
Enemy troops:
The 16th army and the 18th Army defends the city. There are some serious tank divisions outside of the city. In Riga fanatic infantry divisions are deployed. They have support fire from several artillery divisions.
Please notice:
The next two scenarios will be with no-auto-refit. You will gain a lot of pp for refits. 
Sgn. Major Heinz
