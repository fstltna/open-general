Invasion Salerno

September 11, 1943

At the same time the Allies are establishing a foothold near Salerno, German forces are trying to push the Allied invasion back out to sea.

Two Player

13 Turns

Note:  There is an Allied air base on ocean hexes on the western edge of the map.  These represent Sicilian airbases.  It would mean certain death for German air units to attempt to attack these bases.
