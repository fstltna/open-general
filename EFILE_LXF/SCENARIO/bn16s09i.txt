
BERMUDA - 8 MAY 1937

Northeast off Bermuda, North Atlantic

Commander in Chief, Atlantic Fleet informs
The Canadians have asked for cease-fire and retired from war! Now we just have to convince the stubborn Brits - and the Australians, New Zealanders, South Africans, Indians - I forgot someone?
Anyway, we'll see how the British feel after the loss of Bermuda. The Royal Navy is weakened and will hardly gathering significant forces to defend the island. You'll cover the landing operation and, if necessary, fend off counterattacks.
