
BALD EAGLE FOREST - 6 JUL 1944

Altoona, Pennsylvania, United States

At the Bald Eagle Forest a crisis is looming! North of the Juniata River the enemy has silently pulled together strong armored forces and attacks our weak northern flank. Our forces advancing on Lake Erie threaten to be cut off. Turn your forces immediately and catch the enemy attack! Prevent a disaster and don't let the enemy break through to the town of Blain! More forces were ordered to this scene and will arrive later.

GOAL: Take all VH's!

NOTE: All axis nations auxiliaries are under your command. In this mission exceptionally the Americans makes the first move!

Main reinforcements:
4th turn - south, 18th Panzergrenadier-Division
