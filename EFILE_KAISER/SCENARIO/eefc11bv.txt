BRILLIANT  VICTORY
-------------------------------------
Congratulations, General! You held the enemy at bay and defeated his counter-attacks, while our men stormed the Turkish  positions before Jerusalem.

After much bitter fighting, General Allenby entered Jerusalem through the Damascus Gate. The Turks have left the city without great resistance, so the sights of Holy City remain unharmed by this bloody war. Now it's time to relax a few days, before we are planning our next steps.
