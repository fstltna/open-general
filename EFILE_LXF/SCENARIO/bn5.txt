1941-45 STAY ALIVE - RED PARTISANS
Soviet Partisan Campaign

Fight as Red Partisan against the German aggressor in the Baltic states & Belarus deep behind the front line. The campaign starts 1941, short after the German attack in Estonia, as the Soviet NKVD fight against Baltic guerrillas. Defeated by the Germans, the remains of the Baltic occupation forces form the first Soviet Partisan units to fight a guerrilla war against the enemy.
