Gettysburg * V

----{secret telegraph depeche to general}-----
THE WHITE HOUSE; WASHINGTON,

Dear Sir,
the United States congratulate you on this victory ! 
You have saved us from a big threat !
I am sorry, but we cannot grant a rest to you and your men... you are moved further by rail - our situation in the south is difficult...

May God be with you !

THE PRESIDENT OF THE UNITED STATES
-----{end of secret telegraph depeche}----
