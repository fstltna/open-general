After two months of fighting, and after the situation on battlefield swinged back and forth for several times, the Germans were finally able to conquer Narvik. The port became important for future plans and campaigns.

You'll reside in the Far North for quite some time to accomodate yourself with the arctic conditions. You are ordered to train hard and to keep the spirit and endurance of your men high. This doesn't look like a mere occupational duty, especially considering that your air support will be once again strengthened with new machines...
