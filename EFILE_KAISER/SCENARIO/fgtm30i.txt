Prut' Operation, (19-24 May) 1-6 June 1915

Gospodin General-Leitenant, the 9th Army (Lechitsky) has prepared the XXX Corps (Zaikonchovsky) to strike at the Austrian flank on the Prut' River near the town of Nadvodnaya.  Units of the Austrian 2nd Army (Pflanzer-Baltin) - XI (von Korda) and XIII (von Rhemen) Corps - must be eliminated on the southern bank of the river.  Having had sufficient time for preparations, Zaikonchovsky has deployed his forces in full.  You will lead the corps in this counterattack.
