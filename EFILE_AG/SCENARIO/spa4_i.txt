Battle of Acerrae - Spartacus vs. Furius

Nola you have left  in chaos behind you, but your pursuers are already within reach. Furius, sergeant of praetor Publius Varinius, stands with 3,000 men at Acerrea.
Show them who you are …

Historical:
Praetor Publius Varinius was elected by Rome to put down the uprising. His sergeant, Furius, met first on the slave army. With 3,000 men, Furius saw towards an easy victory. But Spartacus had set his troops excellent, and Furius lost the battle.
