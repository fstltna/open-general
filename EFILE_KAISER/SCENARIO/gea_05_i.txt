Latema Nek

After the Battle of Salaita, Col. von Lettow-Vorbeck reorganized the German defenses. The Salaita positions were abandoned and German forces moved to the Latema-Reata Hills.  These stood on the way to Kahe. Major Kraut was once again in command of the German defenders.  The British attack on Latema began on March 11. 

Victory conditions: After 12 turns you must hold 4 VHs for a brilliant victory, 3 VHs for a victory and 2 VHs for a tactical victory.
