You have achieved all objectives, the mission was a complete success. Good work!

For this year, you have done your job! Other units will continue our offensive. For the ongoing operation your troops will give for a few more days flank protection and will then gradually removed from the front line. Your winter camp is located in North Carolina. There you will adapt your forces for winter warfare, so that in early '44 your forces are again operational. And one more word - you have done an excellent job in '43, respect!
