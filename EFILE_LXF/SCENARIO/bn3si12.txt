
ACROSS THE YANGTZE RIVER
6 Mar 1941

Part of Operations in Western Hupei
Early March - Mid-March 1941

Again in March, 1941, the Japanese attacked Western Hupei. On March 6, beginning at 0530, the Japanese artillery started shelling, followed by infantry advance and air support, to across the Yangtze River near Ichang.
