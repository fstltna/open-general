06. SEVASTOPOL 1 (17 December 1941)

To the commanding officer of 22. ID (LL):

The famous and mighty fortress of Sevastopol has been reached. We have hopes to storm the enemy and take the important port before the winter sets in. Higher commander is aware of the status of your division but it is vital to gain complete victory when it is in our grasp.

Your division is deployed in the north. Together with Rumanian units and 132. Infanterie Division you will conduct the main assault towards Sevastopol itself. Diversionary attacks will be made in the southern sector while Rumanian units will hold the mountanious central sector. 

You have orders to attack the enemy and advance as far as possible. The enemy is now fighting from fixed positions and will be hard to defeat.


RULES: 
- Do not upgrade, sell or buy units - nor upgrading transports.
- Replacing lost units by buying new units is allowed. Replacements should be the exact same unit as the lost unit.
