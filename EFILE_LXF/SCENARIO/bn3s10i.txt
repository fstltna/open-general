
SHOUYANG-YUTSE RAILWAY - 6 Sep 1940

Part of Hundred Regiments Offensive
(Chinese victory)
Mid-August - Early December 1940

HQ 1st Corps, Province Shansi
CO General Yoshio Iwamatsu

General, the Communist Chinese have launched a strong coordinated offensive against our cities and the railway lines linking them in North China.  Suspicious activities indicate that an attack in your section is imminent.  Take all necessary measures to prevent any success by the communist Guerrillas.  Several enemy attacks are expected from unknown directions!

ORDER - Capture & protect and VHs!
AUXIN - Railway-Support
NEW    - 1xFtr (29 core units)
