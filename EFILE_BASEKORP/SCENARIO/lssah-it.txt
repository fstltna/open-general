1. SS 'Leibstandarte' Division Campaign
You will take command of Hitler's elite bodyguard from the early days as an elite motorised brigade to the epic battles as a fully fledged panzer division.  You will fight through Poland, Holland, France, the Balkans and the Soviet Union, pushing your men to the edge as you prove their skill in battle.
