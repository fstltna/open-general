1909-26 THE WAR OF MOROCCO 

Version: 1.1
Equipment File: Kaiser General
Number of Scenarios: 10
Player Country: Spain
Campaign Designer: Urica
Playtesters: Guille, Santiago Fuertes, Juankar

After losing the Spanish-American War in 1898 Spain sought to recover some of its lost prestige and glory by expanding its influence in Northern Morocco. However, some of the local Berber tribes were determined not to live under Spain's rule and started a ferocious and bloody war that would last for almost two decades.

Are you the proper commander to crush the Moroccan rebels and restore Spain's international prestige?
