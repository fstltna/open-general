Campaña de Anyera

June 29, 1916

In 1916 the Spanish army, with the help of friendly "cabilas" (Berber tribes) attacked the rebel cabila of the Anyera. For Spain it was extremely important to control the Anyera territoy in order to secure the roads from Ceuta to Tangier and Tetuán.
