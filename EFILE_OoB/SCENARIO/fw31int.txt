Mlawa - Ciechanow

Our first important operation in the North of Poland will start on this area. About the enemy forces, intelligence reports that the enemy have deployed here one of his armies, and a half of another one. 
Your mission is to capture the Mlawa - Przasnysz line, and then, open the way to Warsaw by capturing the key city of Ciechanow. Lufwaffe units from the 1st Luftflotte will arrive to destroy the remaining Polish air units and to help you on the ground. OKH expect the best from you in the campaign, good luck.
