
LAKE MICHIGAN - 9 SEP 1944

South Bend, Indiana, United States

The Michigan Lower Peninsula is cordoned and some caught enemy forces are now trying to escape westward over Lake Michigan. Occupy the ports along the coast and prevent the evacuation of this troops. Air landing units were dispatched to Benton Harbor and will support you.

GOAL: Destroy all enemy units and let not more then 5 enemy units reach the Exit Points!

Main reinforcements:
3-4th turn - northeast, 29th SS-Grenadier-Division
