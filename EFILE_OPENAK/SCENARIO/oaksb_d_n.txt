Nordwind

December 30, 1944

The Germans attack the American and French forces near Strassbourg.  Reinforcements for the Allies will arrive from the west in the vicinity of the villages Rehrbaon, Puberg and La-Petite-Pierre and the city of Saverne throughout the early stages of the battle.

The Germans start with half fuel, no depot units and no city hexes from which they can resupply.  The Germans will need to capture city objective hexes in order to resupply units.
 
2 Players

17 Turns
