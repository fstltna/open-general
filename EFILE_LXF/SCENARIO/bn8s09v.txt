BACKGROUND

Operation goal for the Germans was to occupy Slovenian littoral, Istrian peninsula, part of Kvarner littoral as well as Gorski Kotar to Ogulin in order to link up with Army Group F. Often confused with operation WOLKENBRUCH which followed immediately after probably beacuse they are locally known as Rommel's offensive or October offensive.
