Kholm was relieved on 5th May 1942, but by this time there were heavy losses among the defenders; from the 5500 men trapped in the city, more than 1500 were killed and some 2200 were wounded.

Your battalion was crucial in this mission, but the Pz II tanks are starting to show their weakness. For the upcoming operations you will receive a mix of Pz IIIs, some of them repaired, some of them brand new.
