TACTICAL VICTORY

This time there were no negotiations held and little chivalry shown. The final battle of the Ethiopians war was fought, bitterly, at Gogetti.

The relics of the armies of Sidamo and Bale were outnumbered by the forces facing them, not considering the additional column after columns of encircling troops who were ready to fall on them.

That day Dejaz Beiene Merid and Dejaz Gabremariam died.
