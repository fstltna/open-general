Liberation of Edirne - victory

The Bulgarians are fleeing and Eastern Thrace is ours again. We'll learn from these experiences, and when the next war comes, we'll make sure that we have powerful allies on our side. Then the time for retribution will come.

Historical outcome
During the second Balkan war, the Ottoman Empire recovered Eastern Thrace with little effort, taking advantage of Bulgaria being attacked by Serbia, Greece and Romania.
