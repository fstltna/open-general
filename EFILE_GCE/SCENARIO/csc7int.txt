Batalla del Ebro

20 de Agosto de 1938

Sientese porfavor, pasare a explicarle la situacion. La cosa se ha tornado exageradamente peligrosa en el sector de Gandesa, cerca de la gran curva del Ebro antes de Amposta. Un poderoso ataque a traves del Ebro con lo mejor que tienen los rojos ha pulverizado a nuestra 50 Division. Bien es cierto que eran tropas inexpertas, pero no podemos permitir que los republicanos prosigan mas alla de Gandesa .

Estamos sacando reservas de otros frentes menos importantes, contamos con usted para detener a los rojos y hacer que crucen de nuevo el rio Ebro en direccion opuesta.

Buena suerte, esta vez la necesitara.


Batalla del Ebro

August - October, 1938

The situation is extremely dangerous, we have stopped our offensive over Valencia because the red forces have succesfully crossed the big curve of the river Ebro near to Gandesa (Tarragona). This is a strong attack, the best enemy troops are carrying out this assault; our 50 Division is completely defeat and now it's resisting desperately agaisnt the Republican assaults to Gandesa and Villalva de los Arcos. 

We are sending reinforcements from other less important fronts... the first reinforcements which will arrive are your elite troops, we count on you to stop the Republican offensive. If they get a victory here, the course of the war will turn favorable for the reds, and we cannot allow that!. The Republicans must cross back the river!.

Good Luck, this time you'll need it.
