
KIROVOGRAD - 7 Jan 1944

HQ Army Group South, Ukraine
General Erich von Manstein

Lieutenant General, your division must support a counterattack in the area of Kirovograd. You must strike the Russians boldly! We cannot afford failure in this battle.

AUXIN -  1 Tank-Division and some heavy tank detachments & Air-Support
HINT  - Let the enemy come! The Russians will launch attacks from northwest and southwest directions.
