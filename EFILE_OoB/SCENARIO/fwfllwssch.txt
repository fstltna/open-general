Fall Weiss, the German invasion of Poland,
September-October 1939.

At 4.45 a.m. on September 1, 1939 German forces launch a large scale attack on Poland from the north and southwest, without a declaration of war... the invasion has begun!

Operational Choices:

1: German Third Army, Army Group North
     (General der Artillerie Georg von Küchler)

2: German Eighth Army, Army Group South
    (General der Infanterie Johannes Blaskowitz)

3: German Fourteenth Army, Army Group South
    (Generaloberst Wilhelm List)
