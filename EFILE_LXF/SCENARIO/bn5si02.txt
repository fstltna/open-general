
GERMAN BLITZ
11 JUL 1941

Summer '41, the Nazi war machine stand at the gates of Estonia. A soviet train with important war goods must pass Tartu before the city falls in German hands. Can the soviet defenders stand long enough?

2 players

Order
Allies: Protect the train and hold at last 1 VH for 10 turns.
Axis:   Search and destroy the soviet train or take all VHs in 10 turns.
