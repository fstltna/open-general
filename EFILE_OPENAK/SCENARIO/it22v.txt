EL AlAMEIN OPERATION LIGHTFOOT October 1942 V

The attack in the South proved to be nothing more than a diversion and once Rommel returned to Egypt, our armored forces succeeded in containing the main attack in the North. 

We are running low on fuel and ammo. And we have commited most of our reserve regiments. All we can do now is to wait and see what Monty's next move is.
