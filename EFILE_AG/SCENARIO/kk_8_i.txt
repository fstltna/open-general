AC 102 - Harbingers to the Battle of Aquae Sextiae 
 
Teutobod, leader of the Teutons, chanced the camp of Gaius Marius - can he manage to annihilate the Romans,
or is he at least able to pass their camp?

Notes of historical interest:

The Teutons had crossed the Rhone in summer 102BC and moved downstream the left bank and came finally upon the Roman camp. For three days, the Teutons rushed again and again against the entrenchments, but failed due to the superiority of the Roman siege warfare. 
After some losses the Teutons decided to avoid the camp an go straight towards the Mediterranean and Italy ...
