
GANALE DORIA
6 Jan 1936

A battle on the "southern front" fought during the Second Italo-Abyssinian War. Technically, Ras Desta Damtew launched an Ethiopian offensive against the Italian forces in Somaliland. However, General Rodolfo Graziani carried out his active defense so vigorously that it became an offensive.
