
OVER THE MIUS - 8 Jul 1942

Mius River, Ukraine
CO General Wilhelm List

General, after the hard winter battles, our Army Group A and B are ready to launch the main offensive of this summer! Target is the conquest of the Caucasian oil fields. With the 17th Army you will break through the Soviet front at the Mius River. Then we can advance through the Donez basin on our first target the city Rostov.
