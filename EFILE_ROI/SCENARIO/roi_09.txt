Kremenskaja - 23-08-1942

The russian bridgehead at Kremenskaya has always been a threat to the flank and
back of the 6th army. 22. Panzerdivision zbV and 376. Infanteriedivison are there
to secure the situation.

It´s your task to throw the russian troops back over the Don and interrupt the supply
transports on the road to Stalingrad.

Little help is expected to arrive later from 100th Jäger Division.
