Ofensiva del Ebro

25 de Julio de 1938

El enemigo ha alcanzado el mar Mediterraneo y las cosas no pueden ir peor, tal vez esta sea nuestra ultima oportunidad de encauzar la situacion, general. Se ha concebido un nuevo plan de batalla, esta ocasion es el mas ambicioso de todo cuanto hemos hecho hasta ahora. Nuestro Ejercito Popular ha tomado sigilosamente posiciones en torno al rio Ebro, cerca de Amposta, en Tarragona, con la mision de cruzarlo y romper asi el frente por una zona por la que el enemigo no se lo espera.
El objetivo primario es cruzar el rio y establecer una fuerte cabeza de puente alcanzando la capital de la comarca; Gandesa. Una posible victoria en esta operacion nos permitiria futuros avances que tal vez pudiesen re-equilibrar la situacion.

Buena suerte.

Ofensiva del Ebro

July 25th, 1938

[Handwritten message received by courier]

The enemy has finally reached the Mediterranean sea, and things can't be worse. Maybe this will be our last chance to reverse the fortunes of war, commandante. A new plan has been designed. This offensive is our biggest gamble in the war up to now. People's Army units have silently taken positions along the river Ebro, nearly to the town of Amposta, in the province of Tarragona. The main mission is to cross the great Ebro river and break through the enemy lines here, in a place where an attack is not expected. Your mission is to cross the river, make a bridgehead to allow more troops to cross, and finally reach and conquer the town of Gandesa.

A victory here would allow us further advances. If we are defeated here ... No, failure is too terrible to contemplate.

Buena suerte.
