The Plattensee offensive - March 1945 L

You were not able to penetrate the Russian lines of defense. Retreat towards Vienna and regroup North of the NeusiedlerSee.

HISTORY
Hitler, enraged at the failure of the Platensee attack, orders the SS divisions LSSAH, Das Reich, Totenkopf and Hohenstaufen to remove the honor cuffbands from their uniforms. 6 SS-Panzer Army commander Oberstgruppenfuehrer Joseph (Sepp) Dietrich refuses to pass the order down the ranks.
