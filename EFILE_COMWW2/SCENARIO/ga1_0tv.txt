Note: This is a semi historical scenario, as Operation Gauntlet did not involve any combat, although the Allies did send a force that was prepared for it...

Historical note: Operation Gauntlet was one of the first  Allied ground missions in which the Canadians took an active part. After the evacuation, and the demolition of the radio, meteorological, and coal mining facilities on Spitzbergen, the Allied team then carried out a successful ploy to deter the Germans from flying over the island; they began transmitting fake weather reports indicating low cloud cover and fog shrouding the area. The Germans didn't discover what had happened on Spitzbergen until Sept 5...
