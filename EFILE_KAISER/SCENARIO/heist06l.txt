Kolonel, under your exceedingly feeble leadership, the Estonian army has been nearly decimated.  With communications disrupted, the forces of our Nordic allies, the relief forces of the Kuperjanow Brigade, and the British Baltic fleet continue to resist the Red Army.

With supply lines severely exteded, the Bolsheviks have been unable to secure their gains at Tallinn:  they have been driven back.  With the situation under control (and many officers dead), you miraculously retain your command!  You must attain a victory now!
