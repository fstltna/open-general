You have won, but...

...The Polish uprising was a failure. In fact there was no uprising.

KuK HQ decided to create an independent Legiony Polskie (Polish Legions). Their soldiers were mainly volunteers from the Russian-occupied Kingdom of Poland. 



You will be awarded an artillery unit and 144 pps (at least).
 
Upgrade your initial infantry unit. You can name it "1. pu³k piechoty legionów", or "1. pp".

Buy a second infantry. You can name it as "5. pu³k piechoty legionów", or "5. pp".
 
Artillery can be named as ""1. pu³k artylerii legionów", or "1. pa".
