Chinese campaign, 1884-1900

The Chinese Empire has vowed to leave behind decades of decadence, rebellions and humiliations at the hands of the Western powers. It's time to build a modern, competent army, capable of defending this once glorious country from the predations of the foreign devils.

Follow the Qing Army in its struggle against France, Japan and the Eight Nation Alliance during the last years of the XIX century. Fight for the traditions and the independence of the Empire and build a brighter future for China.
