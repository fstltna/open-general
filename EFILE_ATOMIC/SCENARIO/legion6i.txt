SITUATION: 

Comandante, your men will be part of our attack force that is about to invade Yugoslavia. You should secure the islands and coast of North Dalmatia as well as the hinterland. The distribution of your forces according to the objectives will be the key to success.

ORDERS:

Take all VHs in 12/15/18 turns for a BV/V/TV.

NOTES:

You can buy 1 unit in this scenario.

4 air-transports are available. You may want to deploy your paratroopers at sea to use them.

Naval transports are available too in case you want to embark units later.
