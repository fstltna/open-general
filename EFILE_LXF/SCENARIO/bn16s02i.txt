
THE HUNT - 3 SEP 1936

East off Sydney, Nova Scotia

Commander in Chief, Atlantic Fleet informs
British submarines lying in front of the east coast and threatening our trade routes. We've strengthened monitoring with planes and airships. A blimp has spotted enemy submarines northeast of your position. Protect our merchant ships - find and destroy this subs before they can do any damage.
