Madrid - Battle of Ciudad Universitaria - November, 1936 - L

Stuborn republican resistance and many enemy reinforcements prevented general Varela's troops from capturing the university area. Your forces were of little help. A change of strategy and tactics is required.

HISTORIC EVENTS
November 8th: nationalist forces started the assault on Casa de Campo, which they conquered after a bloddy battle.
November 15th: nationalist forces crossed the river Manzanares and the battle for the university area started.
November 23rd: after a week of heavy fighting in and around the university buildings with heavy losses on both sides, Franco stops the direct attack on the city.
