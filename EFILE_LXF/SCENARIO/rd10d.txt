The Sack Of London (1952)

March 12, 1952

The Red Army, exhausted from more than three months of determined Allied resistance, comes to it's final objective: The British capital of London.  The Allied forces are desperate to prevent the Soviets from capturing this last safehouse of European democracy and have several Free French and German units in addition to some green British troops in their attempt to defend London.

2 Players

30 Turns
