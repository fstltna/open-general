All our efforts were in vain. The Bolsheviks were too strong and numerous, and nothing seemed to work against them.

That was the end of the Komuch. The Bolsheviks were a buch of fanatics, criminals and murderers, but their determination was simply enviable. They would stop at nothing in order to obtain power, and that gave them an advantage over all of us. Just a few days later, a massive Soviet counterattack forced us to retreat down the Volga, towards Samara, with our enemies on our heels.
