Fall Weiss [3 Armee]

Fall Weiss, Poland 1939

-3th German Army, Army Group North-

-General Kuechler-

September, 1939, Germany finally has broken the relationships with Poland, the invasion is on the way. The Polish will try to resist with an outdated army against the fast German Panzerdivisionen. In the 3. Armee you will have the control of the Panzer Division ''Kempf'', the 10th Infanterie Division, and the 1st Cavalry Brigade. Your mission will be open the way to Warsaw from East Prussia
