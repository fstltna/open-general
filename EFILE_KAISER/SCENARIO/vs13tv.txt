Antwerp - The second exit - September 9th 1914 - TV

You stopped the Belgian Army and pushed them back towards Antwerp, but our advance on Paris has been halted. The Allies won the battle of the Marne and our army had to retreat to the Aisne. 

With hopes of a quick capture of Paris gone and your 6.Reserve Division reunited with 5.Reserve Division, it is time to deal with Antwerp.
