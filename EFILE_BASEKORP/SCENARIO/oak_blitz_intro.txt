Open AK Blitzkrieg

Version:  Apr-2019
Number of Scenarios: 23
Player Country: Germany
Equipment file: Basekorp 
Campaign Designer: Llewen
Modified by Brian Gosley aka Puma

You take command from the civil war in Spain, to the "lightning" victories of early World War II and beyond.

Notes:  The intent is to take the original classic SSI Blitzkrieg campaign and add as many of the new features of Open General as possible.
