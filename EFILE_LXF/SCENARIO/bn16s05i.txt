
THE BAHAMAS - 27 NOV 1936

Northwest off New Providence Island, Bahamas

Commander in Chief, Atlantic Fleet informs
A regiment of our marine corps should attack the island of New Providence and seize Nassau, the capital of the Bahamas. Your task is to cover the landing and provide fire support.
