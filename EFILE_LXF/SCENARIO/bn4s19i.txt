
ESCAPE FROM EGYPT - 8 MAY 1943

Military Prison Al-Qabbari, Alexandria

The renowned hero now only a public enemy! Jailed in the military prison of Al-Qabbari you are waiting for your trial because of communist propaganda and insubordination against the Middle East High Command and the Greek government in exile. However, your friends managed to organize your escape to Greece and to provide you with a getaway car. At the airport of Alexandria a plane is ready for take off and alternatively a vessel waiting for you located in the port of Al Muaddiyah. Reach one of these points without to be caught at the control points of the Brits! Friends on the Greek freighters SS Dimitrios Inglessis and SS Thrace monitor the airport and the port for you!

GOAL: Retreat your MSU via Exit Point!
