
PERVOMAYSK - 5 Aug 1941

Pervomaysk, Ukraine
CO General Gerd von Rundstedt

Welcome in Russia, General! It is a special honour, that Italy send the best divisions for supporting the fight against the Bolshevism. Il Duce's desire is that your troops are used at the first front line. Here the actual situation; in the area around Pervomaysk we have encircled strong Russian forces. You will help to destruct this pocket. Do not give away any time!
