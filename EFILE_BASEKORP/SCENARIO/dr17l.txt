Rzhev-Vyazma Operation - Februari 1942 L

The Russian 33rd Army was able to relieve the 39rd Army, 29th Army and the 11th Cavalry Corps. Instead of Russian troops, lots of German divisions are now trapped behind enemy lines. 
A Kamfgruppe of your division will stay on the Eastern front until June 1st, but you are ordered to move to France to prepare the reorganization of Das Reich into a Panzer-Grenadier division.
