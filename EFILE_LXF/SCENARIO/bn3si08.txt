
ATTACK ON NANNING
23 Nov 1939

Part of Battle of Southern Kwangsi
Mid-October - Late February 1940

In November of 1939, the Japanese landed on the coast of Kwangsi and captured Nanning. In this battle, the Japanese successfully cut off Chongqing from the ocean, effectively severing foreign aid to China's war efforts by the sea, rendering Indochina, Burma Road and The Hump as the only way to send aid to China.
