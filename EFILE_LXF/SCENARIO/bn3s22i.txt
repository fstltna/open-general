
AUGUST STROM - 12 Aug 1945

Operation August Storm
(Decisive Soviet victory)
August 1945

HQ 44th Corps, Province Chahar
CO General Hongo Yoshio
 
General, the traitorous Russians have broken the peace treaty and attacked us in Manchuria.  Your Division must defend the region around Solon.  You must stop the Russian Armour!

ORDER - Capture & protect all VHs!
AUXIN - 2 Infantry-Brigades & Air-Support
HINT   - Beware of the strong enemy Air Force!
