3 May 1942

Operations along the Yunnan-Burma Road
Mid-March - Early June 1942

Since the Japanese initiation of the Pacific War, the Japanese captured Maly Peninsula, and Thailand became subjugated to Japan. The Japanese's 15th Corps landed at Rangoon to push north and attempted to cut off the most important China-Burma Road.
